3 Dimensional, 6-DOF, IR head tracking

If you've ever seen TrackIR, you know what this is about. But what if you could build your own for signifciantly less cost? 6 degrees of freedom (triple axis) anaologue head tracking powered by infra red lights and a Sony pS3 eyecam


What you will need:

- 3x IR LED
- 3x 1/2 W resistors
- 1x AA or AAA cell
- Sony PS3 Eyecam
- A soldering Iron
- FreeTrack or OpenTrack
- A little know how, or a handy guide
- A Calculator to work out which resistors you need to go with your LEDs, I used LEDwiz
